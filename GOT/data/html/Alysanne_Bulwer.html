Alysanne Bulwer
Title Lady of Blackcrown
Allegiance House Bulwer
Born In 291 AC[1]
Book(s) A Storm of Swords (mentioned)
A Feast for Crows (mentioned)
A Dance with Dragons (appendix)

Lady Alysanne Bulwer is Lady of Blackcrown and head of House Bulwer. Daughter of Lord Jon Bulwer and Lady Victaria Tyrell, distant cousin of Lord Mace Tyrell. Succeeded her father, after he died of a sickness. She was born in 291 AC[1]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Alysanne is a lady attendant of Margaery Tyrell in King's Landing.[2]

A Feast for Crows

Lady Alysanne and Queen Margaery play come-into-my-castle together. It is one of the things Ser Osney Kettleblack claims is preventing him from seducing Margaery.[3]
