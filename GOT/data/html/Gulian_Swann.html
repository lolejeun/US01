Gulian Swann


Title Lord of Stonehelm
Allegiance House Swann
Born In 259 AC or before[1]
Book(s) A Clash of Kings (mentioned)
A Storm of Swords (mentioned)

Gulian Swann is Lord of Stonehelm and head of House Swann. He is well past forty.[2]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Lord Gulian agrees to meet Ser Davos Seaworth but refuses to join the cause of Stannis Baratheon.[3] During the War of the Five Kings, Lord Gulian remains in his castle pleading illness.[4]

Gulian's eldest son, Ser Donnel, supports first Renly Baratheon and then Stannis. He is captured during the Battle of the Blackwater and kneels to Joffrey Baratheon.[citation needed] Gulian's second son, Ser Balon, joins Joffrey's Kingsguard after the death of Ser Preston Greenfield.[citation needed]

A Dance with Dragons

Clifford Swann is listed as Lord of Stonehelm.[5] It is unknown if this is a mistake or whether Clifford has succeeded Gulian over Gulian's eldest son Donnel.
