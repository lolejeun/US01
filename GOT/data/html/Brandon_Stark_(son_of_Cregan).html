Lord Brandon Stark was a Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark. He was the youngest son of Lord Cregan Stark and Lady Lynara Stark.

History

Brandon succeeded his brother Barthogan Stark as Lord of Winterfell. He was married to Lady Alys Karstark, with whom he had three children: Rodwell, Beron and Arsa Stark. He also had a bastard son, Lonnel Snow, with Lady Wylla Fenn.[1]

He is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.[2]
