Halys Hornwood[N 1] is Lord of the Hornwood and head of House Hornwood. He is the husband of Lady Donella Manderly and the father of a trueborn heir, Daryn, and a natural son, Larence Snow.[3]

Character

Halys is jovial and ever-obliging.[4][5]

Recent Events

A Game of Thrones

Lord Halys goes to Winterfell when Robb Stark calls the banners. He gives gifts to Robb in hopes that he will give him leave to dam the White Knife, hunting privileges north of a ridge, and a holdfast taken from his grandfather.[4] He is sent with Lord Roose Bolton when the northern army splits at the Twins. He is killed at the Battle of the Green Fork.[2]
