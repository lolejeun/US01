Pebbleton
The Iron Islands and the location of Pebbleton[1]

Pebbleton is a village on Great Wyk in the Iron Islands. It contains Pebbleton Tower, the seat of House Merlyn. The hovels that surround the tower are home to roughly seven thousand people, most of them employed in the fisheries.[2]
