Ser Donnel Locke is a knight of House Locke.

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

At some point, Donnel is captured by Lannister forces. He is offered along with Lord Medger Cerwyn in a trade for Tion Frey by Tyrion Lannister.[1]

A Storm of Swords

At some point, Donnel is released from captivity. He attends the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully to Lady Roslin Frey at the Twins. He is among those nobles killed during the Red Wedding, getting hit with crossbow bolts.[2]
