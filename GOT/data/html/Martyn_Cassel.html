Martyn Cassel
Allegiance House Cassel
Culture Northmen
Died In 284 AC, at the Tower of Joy
Book(s) A Game of Thrones (mentioned)
A Clash of Kings (mentioned)

Martyn Cassel was a member of House Cassel and the brother of Ser Rodrik Cassel. Martyn fathered four sons, but only Jory Cassel lived to be a man.[1]

History

A proud man, Martyn fought alongside Lord Eddard Stark during Robert's Rebellion.[2] He died at the Tower of Joy in the Red Mountains of Dorne during a battle with three members of Aerys II Targaryen's Kingsguard.[2]
