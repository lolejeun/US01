Ser Guyard Morrigen, also known as Guyard the Green, is a knight of House Morrigen of the Stormlands. He was named to King Renly's Rainbow Guard.[3]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Guyard is part of Renly Baratheon's army encamped beneath Bitterbridge, and a member of the Rainbow Guard, Renly's equivalent of the Kingsguard.[3] After the parley between the brothers Renly and Stannis Baratheon beneath the walls of Storm's End, Guyard asks for command of the vanguard in the imminent battle between the two forces, but is passed over in favor of Loras Tyrell.[4] When Renly is murdered within his tent before the battle, Guyard is not present.[5]

After Renly's death, Guyard goes over to Stannis. He is present beneath the walls of Storm's End for the parley with Cortnay Penrose.[6] When Stannis' army approaches King's Landing, Guyard is given command of the vanguard.[7] During the Battle of the Blackwater, he is slain by Renly's ghost, who was actually Garlan Tyrell in Renly's armor.[8]
