Characters

Wat is the name of:

  • The Blue Bard, a singer.
  • Wat Whitesmile is a singer in service to House Lannister.
  • Wat (Night's Watch), a member of the Night's Watch.
  • Wat Barleycorn, a farmer in service to House Osgrey.
  • Wet Wat, a farmer in service to House Osgrey, brother of Wat Barleycorn.
  • Wat, a farmer in service to House Osgrey.
  • Wat, an orphan boy.
  • Wat, a member of the crew of the Brazen Monkey.
  • Wat the Hewer, a member of the Poor Fellows during the Faith Militant uprising.

Places

Wat's Wood is the forest located on House Osgrey lands.
