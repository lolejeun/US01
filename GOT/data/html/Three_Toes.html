Three Toes
Allegiance Brave Companions

Three Toes is a member of the Brave Companions.[1]

    • 1.1 A Storm of Swords

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Three Toes is one of the many Brave Companions who remain behind at Harrenhal after several failed foraging runs. He is present when Brienne of Tarth is thrown into the bear pit and raises his sword against Jaime Lannister when he comes back to save her, but Vargo Hoat orders him to stand down.[2]

A Feast for Crows

Three Toes, like many of the Brave Companions, is on the run.[3]
