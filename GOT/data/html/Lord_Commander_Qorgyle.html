Lord Commander Qorgyle was a member of House Qorgyle and was the 996th Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. His given name has not been revealed.

History

Lord Commander Qorgyle entered the Night's Watch some time before Ser Denys Mallister.[2] During his term, Castle Black used to send patrols east towards Eastwatch-by-the-Sea every third day and west towards the Shadow Tower every second day.[3] He once visited Winterfell with an escort of a dozen men, including Mance Rayder, to meet with Lord Eddard Stark.[4]

After Qorgyle's death, Jeor Mormont was named Lord Commander in 288 AC.[1]
