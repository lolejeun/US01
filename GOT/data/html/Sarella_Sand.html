Sarella Sand is the fourth eldest of the Sand Snakes, the bastard daughters of Oberyn Martell.[2] Her mother is the captain of the Feathered Kiss.[3]

Appearance and Character

See also: Images of Sarella Sand

Sarella has a reputation for insatiable curiosity.[4][5] She loves Oldtown.[2]

History

Sarella is Prince Oberyn Martell's fourth daughter. Her mother, the trader captain of the Feathered Kiss,[3] comes from the Summer Isles.[6]

Sarella sometimes played with her cousin, Princess Arianne Martell, at the Water Gardens, although Arianne was always closer to Sarella's half-sister, Tyene Sand.[5]

Sarella once visited the abandoned holdfast Shandystone with her father, Tyene, and Arianne. Interested in the history of the ruins, Sarella turned over rocks and brushed sand off its mosaics.[4]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Sarella Sand, by TheThreeHares

Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne, orders Areo Hotah to take the Sand Snakes into custody, but as Sarella is outside of Dorne, he leaves her be.[2]

Quotes

Areo: What of Sarella? She is a woman grown, almost twenty.
Doran: Unless she returns to Dorne, there's naught I can do about Sarella save pray that she shows more sense than her sisters. Leave her to her... game.[2]

—Areo Hotah and Doran Martell
