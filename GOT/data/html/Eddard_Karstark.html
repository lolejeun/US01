Eddard Karstark, affectionately called Edd by his sister Alys, is the second son of Lord Rickard Karstark.[2] He is named after Eddard Stark.[3]

Recent Events

A Game of Thrones

Eddard accompanies his father and his two brothers Harrion and Torrhen to Winterfell when Robb Stark calls his banners.[3] He is a member of Robb's personal guard during the battle in the Whispering Wood. Eddard and Torrhen are both killed by Ser Jaime Lannister during the battle.[4]
