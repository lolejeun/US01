Longbow Hall
The Vale and the location of Longbow Hall

Longbow Hall is the seat of House Hunter in the Vale. It is located south of the Fingers, east of Heart's Home, and north of Old Anchor.[1] It appears to be located in the foothills between a mountain range and the east of the valley.[2] Willamen is the maester at Longbow Hall.[3]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Lord Gilwood Hunter brings his levies to the Gates of the Moon.[4]
