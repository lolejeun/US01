Ser Raymun Darry is the Lord of Darry and head of House Darry.[1]

History

Raymun's three older brothers all died fighting for the Targaryens in the battle of the Trident.[2] Under Ser Raymun, House Darry secretly kept its loyalty to the Targaryens.[3]

Recent Events

A Game of Thrones

Ser Raymun is present at Darry when Arya Stark is found and brought before King Robert I Baratheon for punishment after the attack on Prince Joffrey Baratheon.[2] Later, he is sent to King's Landing by Lord Hoster Tully, along with Ser Marq Piper, to receive the king's permission to hunt down Ser Gregor Clegane, who had been ravaging their lands. Lord Eddard Stark, the Hand of the King, agrees and sends a party, which includes Raymun, to capture Gregor.[4] He is later killed during an ambush set by Gregor at the battle at the Mummer's Ford.[5] He is succeeded by his son, Lyman.[6]
