Ser Pate of the Blue Fork,[2][3] also known as Ser Pate of Sevenstreams,[4] is a hedge knight from Sevenstreams in the northern riverlands.[5]

History

Pate the hedge knight married Amerei Frey, the eldest daughter of Merrett Frey, after she was caught in the stables of the Twins having sex with three grooms at the same time.[5] Pate's cousin, Tom of Sevenstreams, sang at his wedding to Amerei, but Pate did not want to pay for his singing.[5]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Amerei Frey is widowed because of Pate's death.[6]

A Storm of Swords

Lord Walder Frey tells Robb Stark he had negotiated a marriage between his granddaughter Amerei and Pate, but Pate was then killed by Ser Gregor Clegane during the War of the Five Kings.[4]

A Feast for Crows

When Jaime Lannister visits Castle Darry, Lady Amerei tells how Pate had sworn to kill Gregor when House Frey was still supporting Robb.[3]

Quotes

I married Ami to Ser Pate of Sevenstreams, but the Mountain killed the oaf so I got her back.[4]

– Walder Frey to Robb Stark


I sang at your daughter's wedding. And passing well, I thought. That Pate she married was a cousin. We're all cousins in Sevenstreams. Didn't stop him from turning niggard when it was time to pay me.[5]

- Tom of Sevenstreams to Merrett Frey


I begged him not to go, but my Pate was oh so very brave, and swore he was the man to slay that monster. He wanted to make a great name for himself.[3]

– Amerei Frey to Jaime Lannister
