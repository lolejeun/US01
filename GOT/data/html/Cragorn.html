Cragorn is a member of Euron Greyjoy's crew aboard the Silence.

Appearance

See also: Images of Cragorn

He is tall, with a broad chest tattooed with a bird of prey. His head is shaved and he wears rings of gold, jade, and jet on his arms.[1]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

He was among Euron's party at the kingsmoot, at which he blew the dragon horn to announce Euron's claim to the title of King of the Iron Islands.[1] He later dies. A maester cuts him open and discovers his lungs had been charred and turned black.[2]
