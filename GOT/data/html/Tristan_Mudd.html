Tristan Mudd
Title Lord Commander of the Night's Watch
Allegiance House Mudd
Night's Watch
Book(s) A Storm of Swords (mentioned)

Tristan Mudd was a member of House Mudd who became Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. He is considered one of the worst commanders the Night's Watch has ever had.[1]
