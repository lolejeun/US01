Greenstone
The stormlands and the location of Greenstone.

Greenstone is the seat of House Estermont on the island of Estermont in the stormlands.

Contents

  • 1 History
  • 2 Recent Events
    • 2.1 A Dance with Dragons
  • 3 References

History

King Robert I Baratheon along with Queen Cersei Lannister and some of the court from King's Landing, including Ser Jaime Lannister of the Kingsguard, spent a fortnight at Greenstone. Robert cheated on Cersei with a cousin of his and Cersei believes that it was there that she and Jaime conceived their eldest child Prince Joffrey. Jaime gave the castle the nickname "Greenshit".[1]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

A force of the Golden Company under the command of Marq Mandrake takes over Greenstone.[2]
