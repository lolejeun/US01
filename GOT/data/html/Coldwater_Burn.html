Coldwater Burn
The Vale and the location of Coldwater Burn (red)[1]

Coldwater Burn[2] or Coldwater[3] is the seat of House Coldwater in the Vale. It is located near the mouth of a river west of the Fingers.[2] The castle of Snakewood is nearby beyond a mountain range to the south.[1] According to semi-canon sources, Coldwater is within the northernmost glacial river valley of the snakewood forest.[4]
