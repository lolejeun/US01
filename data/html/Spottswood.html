Spottswood

From A Wiki of Ice and Fire
Jump to: navigation, search

Spottswood is the seat of House Santagar in Dorne. Sylva Santagar is called "Spotted Sylva" because of her freckles, but her friends pretend it is because she is the heir to Spottswood.[1]
