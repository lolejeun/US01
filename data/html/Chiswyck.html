Chiswyck

Chiswyck
Allegiance House Clegane
Died In 299 AC, at Harrenhal
Book(s) A Clash of Kings (appears)
A Storm of Swords (mentioned)

Chiswyck is a man-at-arms in service to Ser Gregor Clegane.

Appearance and Character

See also: Images of Chiswyck

He is old and stoop-shouldered, with a sadistic temperament masked by a jolly personality.[1]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Chiswyck is one of Ser Gregor Clegane's men at arms and is part of the group that captures Arya Stark, Gendry and Hot Pie in the Riverlands. When Arya tries to bite him, he hits her in the face. He sometimes aided the Tickler in torturing the smallfolk.[1] For this, Arya adds him to her prayer kill list.[1]

Chiswyck tells a story about the gang rape of an innkeeper's daughter that Gregor and the men had instigated, laughing as he recalls the details. Arya overhears this, and whispers his name into the ear of Jaqen H'ghar as the first of her three promised deaths. H'ghar, obliging, pushes him off a wall three days later.[2]

Quotes

Hot Pie: I don’t want to go there. There’s ghosts in there.
Chiswyck: Baker boy, here’s your choice. Come join the ghosts, or be one.[1]

– Hot Pie and Chiswyck, on entering Harrenhal
