Rhea Royce

Rhea Royce
Alias Bronze Bitch
Title Lady of Runestone
Allegiance House Royce
Died In 115 AC, at The Vale
Spouse Prince Daemon Targaryen
Book(s) The World of Ice & Fire (mentioned)
The Rogue Prince (mentioned)

Lady Rhea Royce was the ruling Lady of Runestone. She was the first wife of Prince Daemon Targaryen. She died childless. Daemon had no love or affection for her, referring to her as his "bronze bitch", a mocking reference to the ancient armor of House Royce. Rhea likewise had no love for Daemon.[1]

Rhea died after an hawking accident. Runestone passed to her nephew instead of her husband.
