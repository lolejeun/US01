Ryk, better known as Longspear Ryk, is a wildling raider.

Appearance and Character

See also: Images of Ryk

He is homely, with a friendly demeanor.[1] According to Tormund, he is very well-endowed and that is why he is called Longspear. [2]

History

Ryk is from the same village as Ygritte, who thinks of him as a brother.[1]

He broke the arm of Ygritte's first lover after he tried to take her a second time; both of them deemed him a weak partner for her.[1]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Longspear Ryk is among the wildling raiders that chased Jon Snow and his brothers through the Skirling Pass.[3]

A Storm of Swords

After Jon kills Qhorin Halfhand, Rattleshirt orders Longspear Ryk and Ygritte to guard Jon after he proves he is a turncloak.[4]

After Jon is told by Mance Rayder to join Tormund's force, Ryk and Ygritte abandon Rattleshirt and join Tormund too.[5]

Later on, Tormund Giantsbane informs Jon that Longspear stole his daughter Munda.[2]

A Dance with Dragons

Tormund mentions him to Jon, saying that Ryk has now married Munda.[6]
