Dancy is a whore at Chataya's brothel in King's Landing.

Appearance

Dancy has a pug-nose, freckles and long, thick red hair.[1] At some point she changes the color of her hair to honey-coloured blond.[2][3]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Dancy makes a bet with Marei, another whore employed by Chataya, that she could bed Tyrion Lannister or lose her black pearls. She attempts to convince him to take her upstairs, but Tyrion is faithful to Shae and refuses her advances.[1]

A Storm of Swords

Tyrion suggests Dancy to Prince Oberyn Martell.[2]

Anguy reveals he spent the small fortune he had won at the Tourney of the Hand on girls from Chataya's brothel—Jayde, Alayaya and Dancy—roast swan, and Arbor wine.[4]
