Wayfarer's Rest

From A Wiki of Ice and Fire
Jump to: navigation, search

Wayfarer's Rest is the seat of House Vance of Wayfarer's Rest in the Riverlands.

Contents

  • 1 Recent Events
    • 1.1 A Game of Thrones
    • 1.2 A Storm of Swords
  • 2 Behind the Scenes
  • 3 References

Recent Events

A Game of Thrones

Karyl Vance becomes Lord of Wayfarer's Rest after the death of his father in the Battle of the Golden Tooth.[1]

A Storm of Swords

After the Battle of Oxcross, captives such as Ser Lymond Vikary are held here.[2]

Behind the Scenes

Wayfarer's Rest is a reference by George R. R. Martin to Liane the Wayfarer from The Dying Earth by Jack Vance.
