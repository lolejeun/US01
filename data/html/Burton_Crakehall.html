Ser Burton Crakehall is a knight of House Crakehall. He is the younger brother of Lord Roland Crakehall.

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

As part of Tywin Lannister's army in the riverlands, Burton pursues the men of Lord Beric Dondarrion and finally kills the elusive Lightning Lord.[1] Later he and his column of soldiers are slaughtered in an ambush led by a resurrected Lord Beric and his men.[2][3]
