Ser Kyle, known as the Cat of Misty Moor and Kyle the Cat, was a hedge knight from Misty Moor.[1]

Appearance

Kyle was stout and had red facial hair.[1]

History

Ser Kyle once served House Caswell. He claimed to have saved Lord Armond's life during the First Blackfyre Rebellion. He made the first sword for Armond's three-year-old son, Joffrey, carving a wooden sword out of pine.[1]

Ser Kyle was among the small group of hedge knights headed to Lord Ambrose Butterwell's wedding at Whitewalls. He hoped to take part in the wedding tourney and possibly win himself a place in service to a lord. His first joust had him face Lord Joffrey Caswell. Kyle threw the joust in an effort to win a place with House Caswell. Joffrey wanted no part of Kyle, though he did take his arms and armor.[1]
