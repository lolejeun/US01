Allyria Dayne
Allyria Dayne, by Rae Lavergne©
Allegiance House Dayne
Culture Dornish
Book(s) A Storm of Swords (mentioned)

Allyria Dayne is a member of House Dayne, and according to semi-canon sources the younger sibling of Ser Arthur Dayne and Lady Ashara Dayne.[1][2] She is the aunt of the young Edric Dayne, Lord of Starfall, whose father was her older brother.[3] She is betrothed to Beric Dondarrion, Lord of Blackhaven.[3][1][2]
