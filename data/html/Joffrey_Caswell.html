Joffrey Caswell
Title Lord of Bitterbridge
Allegiance House Caswell
Born In 190 AC or 191 AC[1]
Book(s) The Mystery Knight (appears)

Joffrey Caswell was Lord of Bitterbridge and head of House Caswell during the reign of Aerys I Targaryen. At the time of the Second Blackfyre Rebellion he was twenty years of age. He was thin, a poor rider and even worse lance.[2]

History

Lord Joffrey attended the Whitewalls tourney. After falling asleep in his wine the night before, Lord Joffrey faced Ser Kyle in his first joust of the tourney. He defeated Ser Kyle in the first tilt.[2]
