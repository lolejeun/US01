Lord Rodwell Stark was Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark. The eldest son of Lord Brandon Stark and Lady Alys Karstark. He married Lady Myriame Manderly, but had no issue, his brother Beron followed him as Lord of Winterfell.[1]

He is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.[2]
