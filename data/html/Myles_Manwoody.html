Ser Myles Manwoody is a knight of House Manwoody. He is the brother of Dagos Manwoody, Lord of Kingsgrave.[1]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Myles accompanies his brother, Lord Dagos, and his two nephews Mors and Dickon as part of the entourage of Prince Oberyn Martell and travels to King's Landing for the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell.[2]
