Ser Arron Qorgyle is the second son of Lord Quentyn Qorgyle.[1]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

He is part of the escort accompanying Prince Oberyn Martell to King's Landing for the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Lady Margaery Tyrell.[2]

Ser Arron helps Prince Oberyn donning his battle armor for Tyrion Lannister's trial by combat.[3]
