King Brandon Stark, better known as Brandon the Burner, was the son of Brandon the Shipwright.[1]

History

When his father was lost at the Sunset Sea, King Brandon set fire to all of his father's ships in grief. This earned him his nickname.[2] It was the last time the North had any strength at sea.[3]
